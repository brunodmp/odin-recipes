Apple Pie

Description

You may not be Snow White, but you know you love an good old Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 4 big apples
  • 2 cup flour
  • 200g butter, cold, saltless
  • Cinnamonn
  • 1 Vanilla Pod or 1 coffespon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 box heavy cream
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • 100g breadcrumbs
  • Sugar
  • 1 pint Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients of the crust and beat it until get's uniform
  2. Store 1/3 of it for the top
  3. open the crust in the form and bake it for 10 min or until it gts crunchy
  4. slice the apples and mix all the filling ingredients
  5. Fill the bottom of the crust with breadcrumbs
  6. Fill with the filling
  7. Put the top
  8. Bake until it gets golden
  9. Enjoy!
